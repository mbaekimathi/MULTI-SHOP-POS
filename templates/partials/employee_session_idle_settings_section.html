Employee session timeout

Automatically sign employees out of the back-office portal after a period of inactivity. Shop till and POS screens are not affected.

Employees see a warning one minute before sign-out (or 15 seconds for very short timeouts).

Applies to the employee portal only — dashboard, stock, HR, analytics, and similar pages. Active selling on shop POS is excluded so tills are not interrupted.
